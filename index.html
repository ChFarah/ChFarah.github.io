About me

Since September 2023, I am an associate professor (maître de conférence) in machine learning at Jean Monnet University in the Hubert Curien Laboratory in the data intelligence group. I'm also a member of the the Inria project-team MALICE. In 2022, I obtained my Ph.D. in machine learning at the Laboratoire d'Informatique et des Systèmes (LIS) of Marseille in the Qarma group.

Research interests

My research interests in machine learning include statistical learning theory, kernel methods, signal processing, and more recently physics-guided machine learning.
